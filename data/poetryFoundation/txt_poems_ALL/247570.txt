Katharine  Coles
****!****
Kept in Mind
****!****

Hildegard believed
A woman's brain drew

Heat to itself, drew seed
From a man all the way up

The spine's long stair
The stake bracing the spine

Licking all the way orange,
Red, blue -- shut it -- 

And why not? My brain has been
Hungry all this time.
