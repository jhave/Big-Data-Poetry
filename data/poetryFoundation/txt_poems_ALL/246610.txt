Amy  Fleury
****!****
First Morel
****!****

Up from wood rot, 
wrinkling up from duff 
and homely damps, 
spore-born and cauled 
like a meager seer, 
it pushes aside earth 
to make a small place 
from decay. Bashful, 
it brings honeycombed 
news from below 
of the coming plenty 
and everything rising.
