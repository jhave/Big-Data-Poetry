Spin
****!****

I've a friend in possession of
a philosophic spin;
if should I speak of art,
theology, 
the universe,
or whim, 

he thinks I speak of him.

This enduring tic, indicative
of universal spins,
theology, art
and whim,
nonetheless
makes

conversation grim.
