That dream of a cricket 
in the dark of the night
at the foot 
of the gallows tree. 

Virtuous 
cricket. Little, hopeful
heart-
shaped face 
lit up by the moon. 

Little, hopeful, insistent
song 
about the future
sung 
to a hanged man's boots.
