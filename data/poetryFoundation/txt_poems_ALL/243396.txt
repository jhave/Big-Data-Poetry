Langston  Hughes
****!****
God
****!****

I am God--
Without one friend,
Alone in my purity
World without end.

Below me young lovers
Tread the sweet ground--
But I am God--
I cannot come down.

Spring!
Life is love!
Love is life only!
Better to be human
Than God--and lonely.


Originally appeared in the October 1931 issue of Poetry magazine.
