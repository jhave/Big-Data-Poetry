The Soul
****!****

It disappeared. 
It reappeared
as chimney smoke 
that burnt through carcasses
of swallows stilled,
and that it portrayed no will
was why I followed that smoke
with this pair of eyes.

It was that it didn't need
or require my belief
that I leant upon it 
as a tired worker
upon 
a gate.
