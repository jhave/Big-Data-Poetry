Dorothea   Grossman
****!****
Love Poem
****!****

In a lightning bolt
of memory,
I see our statue of Buddha
(a wedding gift from Uncle Gene)
which always sat
on top of the speaker cabinet.
When a visitor asked,
"So, does Buddha like jazz?”
you said, "I hope so.
He's been getting it up the ass
for a long time.”
