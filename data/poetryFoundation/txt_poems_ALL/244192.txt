Miser Time
****!****

Miser time grows
profligate near the 
end: unpinching 
and unplanning,
abandoning the
whole idea of
savings. It's hard
to understand
but time apparently
expands with its
diminishing. The 
door thrown wide
on sliding hills of high-
denomination bills and 
nothing much to buy.
