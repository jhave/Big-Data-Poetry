River Politics

I spit my smack,

Jim slugs his Jack,

Rob stews his lack, 

Carey prepares his rack,

herons hunker on blowdowns,

deer wait on high moon for their rounds,

and the campfire

might as well be an empire

we all

watch dissolve 

(in the slough, a carp roll, a splash)

into ash.
